Dynamics 365 Business Central

An all-in-one business management solution engineered SMBs and growing startups. It connects financials, sales, and inventory into a single platform to streamline processes and scale your business ecosystem.

Dynamics 365 Finance & Operations

An enterprise-grade ERP platform built for large organizations managing global growth. It unifies high-volume commerce and multi-country compliance into a cohesive operations management environment.

Dynamics 365 Supply Chain

Automate your entire supply chain process with Dynamics 365 Supply Chain Management. Boost efficiency, cut costs, enhance accuracy, gain real-time insights, and improve customer service.

Dynamics 365 Sales

Get a collaborative workspace built to transform your quote-to-cash process. By providing a 360-degree view of the customer journey, it enables sales teams to track pipelines, automate daily tasks, and leverage built-in insights to close deals faster.

Dynamics 365 Finance

Take control of complex financial operations. Automate routine processes, reduce investment risks, and guide your business strategy with accurate, real-time financial analytics.

Dynamics 365 Customer Service

Empower your customer support agents with built-in AI insights, structured knowledge bases, and omni-channel tracking routing to deliver exceptional, personalized service encounters.

Dynamics 365 Human Resources

Optimize personnel administrative procedures, aggregate strategic workforce statistics, improve performance compensation planning structures, and maximize self-service leave management workflows.

Dynamics 365 Field Service

Transition tracking metrics effortlessly from reactive to proactive maintenance workflows with embedded IoT integrations, intelligent resource allocation logic, and mobile field utilities.

Advanced Payroll for BC

A customized payroll and HR extension designed to bridge the functional gap in ERP setups. It internalizes complex salary calculations, local tax compliance, and automated ledger postings directly within your BC to eliminate manual data entry.

Purchase Requisition

A procurement add-on that embeds custom request gates and automated approval matrices into Business Central. It streamlines the entire internal request-to-order workflow while maintaining strict budget enforcement and audit trails.

Visitor Management

An operational safety extension built to track guests, assets, and security guard shifts. It logs incoming visitors and their items, automates staff notifications, and enforces a strict check-out verification process for assets leaving the facility.

Leave Management in BC HR Module

A specialized HR extension that automates time-off tracking in Business Central. It features an employee self-service portal, manages multi-tier approvals, and keeps leave balances synchronized with your payroll.

Azure IoT Edge Security
